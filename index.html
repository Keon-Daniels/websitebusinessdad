About the Book

The Last Four Visions of Daniel is the result of an inquisitive mind and the yearning of a young Christian to understand a prophecy, the popular interpretation of which just didn t seem to fit the declarations made in the prophecy. Study along as author Thomas A. Daniels examines the seventy weeks prophecy found in the ninth chapter of Daniel, exploring the idea that all four visions in the latter part of Daniel are the revelation of one message staged over four visits by the angel Gabriel. Prepare to question all you know with regard to the visions of Daniel. Based upon what is currently said about them, these are indeed stunning revelations.
